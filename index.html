About the Market

Join us for an alternative gaming market featuring board games, card games, and handcrafted items. Discover unique gaming experiences and connect with fellow enthusiasts in our welcoming community event.

Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just starting your journey, The Side Quest Market offers something special for everyone in the gaming community.

Venue Information

Information about venue accessibility, facilities, amenities, and event policies at Greasley Sports Centre

Accessibility & Access

Information about wheelchair accessibility, disabled facilities, and access features at the venue

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, the venue has ramp access and is completely on one level, making it fully accessible for wheelchair users.

Are there disabled facilities available?

Yes, we have disabled toilets on-site and disabled parking spaces available near the entrance.

Facilities & Amenities

Information about food options, parking, and family facilities available at the venue

What food and drink options are available?

We have food stalls on-site offering a variety of options. There are also nearby pubs and shops within walking distance for additional dining choices.

Is parking available?

Yes, there is parking available at the venue, including designated disabled parking spaces near the entrance.

Are there facilities for families with children?

Absolutely! There will be a kids area in the gaming room along with a public outdoor kids play area and on-site toilets. The event is family-friendly and welcomes all ages.

Event Policies

Information about event policies including re-entry rules and age suitability

Can I leave and re-enter the event?

Yes, re-entry is allowed with your wristband. Please keep your wristband on throughout the day to ensure smooth re-entry.

Is this event suitable for all ages?

Yes, The Side Quest Market is a family-friendly environment that welcomes all ages. We have activities and vendors suitable for both children and adults.
